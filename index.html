Discover new favorite places hands-free

Feeling lost and completely independent in a new city is great. Our application uses geolocation to play you recorded messages via your earbuds when you are close to an interesting object, spot or location.

An innovative way to travel

Using Echotags, you will get tailor-made information just for you, exactly when it is useful. You can download handmade sets, prepared by humans, in advance and then use them during your trip. Everything takes place offline, so you don't need to worry about your data plan.

Share tidbits about your favorite spots

Echotags is not only about new places. Rediscover your surroundings using the experiences of different people. You can play, save, record, and share your audio tags with the whole world. Possibilities are almost unlimited. Guidebooks, recommendations, voice journals, city exploring games, re-emerging trips or leaving a message on the spot are just a few examples.

Why are we doing this?

Last year I decided to go for a summer holiday with my brother to Rome in Italy. We wanted to visit a city and feel not like tourists but rather like locals who wanted to experience everyday life there. But of course, we also wanted to see few of landmarks, enchanting places, locations that we will remember. There was one problem. We hate guidebooks and planning holidays. We decided to do something with it.

We came into something that we called Echotags. We are builiding a service that helps people who like to travel. Something where anybody can record their own geo-localised audio files with a description of the place. Anybody can listen to those files once approaches their geo-coordinates. The trick is to do it completely hands-free. Your phone will be all time in your pocket.
