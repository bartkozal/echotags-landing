An innovative way to travel

Feeling lost and completely independent in a new city is great. Using Echotags, you will get tailor-made information just for you, exactly when it is useful. Our application uses geolocation to play you recorded messages via your earbuds when you are close to an interesting object, spot or location. You can download handmade sets, prepared by humans, in advance and then use them during your trip. Everything takes place offline, so you don't need to worry about your data plan.

Share tidbits about your favorite spots

Echotags is not only about new places. Rediscover your surroundings using the experiences of different people. You can play, save, record, and share your audio tags with the whole world. Possibilities are almost unlimited. Guidebooks, recommendations, voice journals, city exploring games, re-emerging trips or leaving a message on the spot are just a few examples. You can make them private, share with others for free or sell them.

Why are we doing this?

Traveling is one of the most exciting human activities. We are travelers too. We love to enjoy new places, explore cities, checking out where the best food is or the most beautiful landmarks are. But there is one major drawback which we personally hate; once you go to a new spot, you usually buy a guidebook, check the Internet in advance or go to a tourist information center. The problem is that you then have to carry all those unhandy guidebooks. When you need them the most, they are always inside a bag or a rucksack. Constantly checking out phones or tablets is not a super convenient option either. You would just like to experience new places, without any disturbances, wouldn't you?

We make Echotags to deliver you audio insights, created by other passionate people, which you can listen to during your trips. You can freely choose topics that are interesting for you, and start enjoying new places, hands-free, in a completely new way.
